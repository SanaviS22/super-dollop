Campus Life

Why Fernwood?



Fernwood Elementary is a great school with friendly teachers who always care about you and your progress. A spacious outdoor school, the biggest elementary school in the district, lines walk fluently and everyone can get to class easy and on time. With easy to remember rules that keep kids safe, students, parents, and teachers know thier kid is safe. The education is good, teachers using different resources to help her/his students learn.



Classrooms have every thing neatly arranged with textbooks, journals, and workbooks nearby and accessible. The desks have places to store your books and supplies.

Everything is laid out on 1 floor, with sidewalks instead of hallways. Trees are growing next to the classroom, and there is a forest near the playground, which makes it scenic and beautiful.

What's New?

Fun Friday

Fun Friday - Fun And Responsibility

With Fun Friday, Kids get a break from the study, and the teachers don't need to worry. If all the kids do thier homework and classwork, as a class, their last school hour of friday is free time. It helps teachers, and lets students learn.


Class Jobs

Class Jobs

With Class Jobs, Kids feel good about helping the teacher, and the teachers have a little less to worry about. Everyone is doing a little favor, and the kids feel pride, like they are the important one being asked an important favor.
